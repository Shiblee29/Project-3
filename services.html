Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my session?

For weddings, we recommend booking 6-12 months in advance to secure your date, especially during peak wedding season (May-October). For portrait sessions, 2-4 weeks in advance is usually sufficient, but popular seasons like fall may require more lead time.

What happens if there's bad weather on the day of my outdoor shoot?

We monitor weather conditions closely before outdoor sessions. If severe weather is forecasted, we'll work with you to reschedule at no additional cost. For weddings, we always have backup plans and locations for photos in case of inclement weather.

How long until I receive my photos?

For portrait sessions, you can expect your online gallery within 2 weeks. Wedding photos are typically delivered within 4-6 weeks. Commercial projects have varying timelines based on the scope, which we'll discuss during consultation.

Do you provide raw, unedited files?

We don't provide raw, unedited files as part of our packages. Our editing process is an essential part of our artistic style and professional service. However, if you have specific editing preferences, we're happy to discuss them before your session.

Do you travel for photography sessions?

Yes! We love to travel for photography sessions. Local travel within 30 miles is included in our packages. For destinations beyond that radius, additional travel fees may apply. We're also available for destination weddings and events worldwide.
